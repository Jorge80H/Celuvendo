## Usage Guidelines

1. **Brand names and technologies:** Keep in English (e.g., "Gorilla Glass", "Dolby Atmos", "OIS")
2. **Standard abbreviations:** Keep in English (e.g., "MP", "NFC", "GPS", "USB-C")
3. **Technical terms widely understood:** Can keep in English (e.g., "AMOLED", "HDR", "4K")
4. **General descriptions:** Use Spanish (e.g., "pantalla", "cámara", "batería")
5. **Colombian preferences:** "Parlantes" vs "Altavoces" - both acceptable, "audífonos" preferred over "auriculares"

## Context-Specific Usage

### In Technical Specs Lists
- Use Spanish labels with English values when standard: "Procesador: Snapdragon 8 Gen 2"
- Keep technical abbreviations: "RAM: 8GB", "Batería: 5000 mAh"

### In Marketing Copy
- Mix Spanish descriptions with recognizable English tech terms
- Example: "Pantalla AMOLED de 120Hz para una experiencia ultra fluida"

### In Box Contents
- Full Spanish preferred: "Cable USB Tipo-C", not just "USB-C cable"
- Be explicit about what's NOT included: "No incluye adaptador de corriente"
