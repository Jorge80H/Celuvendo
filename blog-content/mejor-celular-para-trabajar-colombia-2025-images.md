## Notes for Image Generation:
- All images should feel authentic to Colombian context (urban settings, skin tones diversity, local landmarks if visible)
- Avoid overly staged or stock-photo feel - prefer realistic workplace scenarios
- Color palette should be professional but vibrant (reflecting Colombian energy)
- Products shown should be modern smartphones but not specific branded models (to avoid trademark issues)
- Lighting should be natural and professional, not harsh or overly processed
- Include subtle visual cues of productivity and success (person smiling, apps showing positive results, etc.)
- For infographics: Use clear, accessible design with good contrast for readability
