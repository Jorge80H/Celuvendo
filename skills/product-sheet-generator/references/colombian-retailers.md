## Price Research Strategy

### For Complete Market Analysis:
1. **Start with:** Alkosto (most comprehensive, competitive)
2. **Cross-reference:** Éxito, Falabella
3. **Check carriers:** Claro, WOM (unlocked prices)
4. **Review marketplace:** MercadoLibre (market floor)
5. **Verify official:** Brand website (MSRP)

### Typical Price Hierarchy:
1. **Highest:** Official brand stores, Falabella
2. **Mid-range:** Alkosto, Éxito, major carriers
3. **Competitive:** WOM, Ktronix, specialist stores
4. **Lowest:** MercadoLibre (marketplace), promotions

### When to Use Each Source:
- **PVP Reference:** Official brand stores
- **B2C Competitive:** Alkosto (primary), Éxito (secondary)
- **5G Availability:** Claro, Movistar (carrier exclusives)
- **Market Floor:** MercadoLibre
- **Specialist Models:** Marketplace Móvil, Los Distribuidores

## Important Notes

- **Promotions:** Prices change frequently, note date when recording
- **Availability:** Check stock status - "agotado" is common
- **Regions:** Prices may vary by Colombian region
- **Import Duty:** Grey market phones may be cheaper but no warranty
- **Black Friday/Cyber Monday:** Major sales events (November)
- **Day Without VAT:** Government tax-free shopping days (varies)
